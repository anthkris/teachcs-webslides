The Steps

  • 01. Syllabus

    If you're more comfortable starting here, you can. Craft measurable goals and objectives.

  • 02. Action Mapping

    We'll take your initial thoughts and analyze them in terms of creating action/activity oriented experiences.

  • 03. Course Content

    Together, we'll work through the nitty gritty and plan out all the content that will make the course sections ready to ship.

  • 04. Assets

    Last, we'll use a spreadsheet listing out all of the individual assets we need to create. This will help us craft a system to get stuff done together.

Assuming that we've decided that training really is the answer, I can begin with step 2 of the process: Identify what people need to do. Emphasis on applicable, action-oriented experiences will not only help set our PD offerings apart from the hundreds of online courses that consist of video, video, quiz, but it also makes the learning experience more meaningful, memorable, and motivational.
